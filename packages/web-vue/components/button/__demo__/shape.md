## zh-CN

按钮分为 `square` - **长方形（默认）**、`circle` - **圆形**、`round` - **全圆角**三种形状。

---

## en-US

Button is divided into three shapes: `square` - **rectangular (default)**, `circle` - **circle**, and `round` - **full rounded corner**.
