## zh-CN

分页分为 `mini`、`small`、`medium`、`large` 四种尺寸。

---

## en-US

The pagination is divided into four sizes: `mini`, `small`, `medium`, and `large`.

---

      <a-radio value="medium">Medium</a-radio>
      <a-radio value="large">Large</a-radio>
