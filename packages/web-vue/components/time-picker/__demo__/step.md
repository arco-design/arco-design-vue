## zh-CN

通过设置 `step`，可以定制需要显示的时、分、秒的步长。

---

## en-US

By setting `step`, you can customize the step length of the hour, minute, and second.

---

```vue
<template>
  <a-time-picker
    defaultValue="10:25:30"
    :step="{
      hour: 2,
      minute: 5,
      second: 10,
    }"
    style="width: 194px;"
  />
</template>
```
