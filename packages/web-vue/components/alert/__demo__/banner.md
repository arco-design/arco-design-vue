## zh-CN

通过设置 `banner`，可将警告提示作为顶部公告使用（去除边框和圆角）。

---

## en-US

By setting `banner`, the warning can be used as the top announcement (removal of borders and rounded corners).

---

```vue
<template>
  <a-alert banner center>This is an info alert.</a-alert>
</template>
```
